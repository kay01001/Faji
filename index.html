Step‑by‑step process

1. Choose an exchange

We recommend Binance, Coinbase, Kraken, or Bybit. Compare fees, security, and available coins. Always use official links.

2. Deposit funds

Link your bank account, credit card, or use P2P. Minimum deposits vary – typically $10‑50. Wait for confirmation.

3. Buy crypto

Go to the trading pair (e.g. BTC/USDT). Enter amount and buy. You now own crypto on the exchange.

4. Transfer to private wallet

For true ownership, move funds to a wallet you control (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, Ledger). Always triple‑check the address.
